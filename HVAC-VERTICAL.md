# HVAC Contractors - Vertical Chosen ✅

## Target Customer Profile

**Who:** Independent HVAC contractors (1-10 employees)
**Revenue:** $200K-$2M annually
**Pain Points:**
- Seasonal scheduling chaos (summer AC rush)
- Emergency calls interrupting planned work
- Following up on estimates (50%+ get forgotten)
- Managing equipment service history per customer
- No good CRM tailored to HVAC workflow

## Pricing Strategy
- **Starter:** $99/mo (up to 100 customers, basic features)
- **Pro:** $149/mo (up to 500 customers + AI follow-ups)
- **Premium:** $199/mo (unlimited + route optimization)

## MVP Features (HVAC-Specific)

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2):
- Contact/customer database with equipment tracking
- Service history per property
- Basic scheduling

### Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4):
- Estimate tracking and automated follow-ups
- Mobile-responsive UI (techs use phones in field)
- Seasonal maintenance reminders

### Phase 3 (Weeks 5-6):
- AI-powered follow-up suggestions
- Route optimization for service calls
- Equipment replacement predictions

## UI/UX Principles for HVAC

1. **Mobile-First:** Techs use phones 90% of the time
2. **One-Tap Actions:** Quick job completion, photo upload
3. **Offline-Capable:** Must work in basements/attics with no signal
4. **Simple Navigation:** 3 taps max to any feature
5. **Big Touch Targets:** Gloves-friendly interface
